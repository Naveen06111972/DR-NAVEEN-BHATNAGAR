Professional Summary

Prof. (Dr.) Naveen Bhatnagar is a distinguished academician with over 25 years of experience in Industry & Education. He has worked as Associate Dean SOET, Associate Director DCPD at Chandigarh University, and Director/Manager across various Universities, Colleges and Corporate organisations. He is associated with AIMA (All India Management Association, New Delhi) as Expert/Observer/SME, SPJIMR (S.P. Jain Institute of Management & Research) as a member of their Expert Panel since May 2023, and NIRA (No Droit Indian Robotics Assistant, Bangalore) as a member of their expert panel.


Presently associated with Torah Learning Solutions as Senior Manager Training, imparting Trainings/Placement in Galgotia University, Greater Noida since September 2025. Core expertise spans AI/ML/Deep Learning, Business Analytics, Campus Recruitment Training, Curriculum Development, and Institutional Building.
